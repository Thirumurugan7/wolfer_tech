Our Campus


CIIC Madurai Campus

The CIIC Madurai Campus is a 10,000 sq.ft facility located at Azhagarkoil, Madurai. Currently around 16 Incubatees have been incubated at the Campus.

Crescent Innovation & Incubation Council, Madurai Campus initiated on November 2021 for supporting Start-ups and MSMEs in the Tier 2 & 3 Cities. CIIC Madurai currently incubated 20+ Start-Ups and having 10,000 Sq.Ft facility for Co-Working Space.

We are working for Five different sector:
Agribusiness Food & Nutraceutical Clean Tech Renewable Energy Eco Friendly Products

Agribusiness :

We are supporting Farmer, Farmer Producer organization and Cluster formation for the MSMEs by engaging innovative start-ups.

Food & Nutraceuticals :

We are acting as a SINGLE WINDOW SYSTEM for Food and Nutraceuticals Start-ups & MSMEs to support from FARM to FORK Model with all kind of recent technologies.

Clean Tech (Waste Management) :

We are working with the Waste Management Start-ups who are all working on Solid Waste Management, Batteries Management, E – Waste Management and Bio Medical Waste Management

Renewable Energy :

We are supporting innovative Renewable energy Start-ups in Wind power, Solar Power, Biomass/Co-generation, Small Hydro Power, Waste to Energy and Large Hydro.

Eco-Friendly Products :

We are supporting SHGs, Tribals and others in improvise their business model and taking them to the International Market..
